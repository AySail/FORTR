Welcome to Our Blue Planet

Discover the wonders of Earth — the only known planet teeming with life in the solar system. From vast oceans to lush forests and towering mountains, Earth is a vibrant and dynamic world full of incredible diversity. Join us as we explore its beauty and learn how to protect our home for future generations.

Fun Facts About Earth

  • Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only one known to support life.
  • About 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water.
  • Earth's atmosphere is made up mainly of nitrogen and oxygen.
  • The planet's magnetic field protects us from harmful solar radiation.

Ongoing Earth Research

Scientists continuously monitor Earth’s climate, ecosystems, and geology using satellites, ocean probes, and land-based sensors to better understand environmental changes and to protect natural resources.

Why Protect Earth?

Earth provides everything necessary for human survival — clean air, water, and food. Protecting its environment ensures a healthy future for all living beings and preserves its natural beauty.

Human Impact

While humans have transformed the planet in many ways, efforts are underway globally to reduce pollution, combat climate change, and promote sustainable living practices.

Water on Earth

Water sustains all known life on Earth. It cycles through the environment in complex ways — through rain, rivers, glaciers, and oceans — supporting ecosystems and human civilization alike.

Latest News

New studies highlight the urgent need to address climate change. Advances in renewable energy, conservation efforts, and international cooperation offer hope for a sustainable future.
